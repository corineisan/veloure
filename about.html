Who We Are

Born from a passion for beautiful living

Velouré was founded in 2012 by artisans who believed furniture should tell a story. We chose hand selection over automation, natural materials over synthetics, and longevity over trends.

Every piece leaves our workshop carrying the care of the people who made it — crafted with sustainably sourced woods, natural fabrics, and non-toxic finishes.

How We Work

Our craft process, step by step

01
Design & Concept
Every piece begins as a sketch — inspired by nature, architecture, and the needs of real homes.
02
Material Selection
We hand-pick every plank and fabric. Only materials meeting our sustainability standards make it through.
03
Handcraft & Build
Skilled artisans shape, join, upholster, and finish each piece by hand. No shortcuts, ever.
04
Quality Check
Every piece passes a rigorous inspection before leaving — structure, finish, and alignment verified.

Our Materials

Only the finest materials touch our hands

We travel, test, and build long-term supplier relationships to ensure every material is beautiful, durable, and environmentally responsible.

  • FSC-certified solid oak, walnut & ash
  • Natural linen, wool & organic cotton upholstery
  • Water-based, non-toxic finishes
  • Recycled metal hardware & sustainable packaging
  • Certified by European eco-design standards
